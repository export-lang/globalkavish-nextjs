**General rules for all clips**
- Shoot **horizontal (16:9)** for section/background use unless a vertical is noted.
- Hold the camera **very steady** (gimbal/tripod). Slow, deliberate moves only — no fast pans.
- Prefer **4K** capture; deliver at least **1080p**. Good, even lighting.
- Keep each clip short and loopable; we trim and mute. No on-screen text or logos.
- No factory signage, no LYCA branding, no faces you don't have consent for.
